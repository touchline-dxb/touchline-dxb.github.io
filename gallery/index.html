Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Touchline Sports Academy located in Dubai?

Our main location is inside Al Ahli Club, Al Nahda 1, behind Al Mulla Plaza — near Stadium Metro Station. We also have a second location in Al Warqa 1, inside International Academy, Dubai.

Can I watch my child's football training session?

Yes! Parents are welcome to watch sessions from our viewing areas. We encourage family involvement as it motivates young players and helps parents see their child's progress firsthand.

How do I enroll my child in the best football academy in Dubai?

Simply visit our Register page and book a FREE trial session. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your spot. No commitment required for the trial.
