About Us

TSA Overview

Touchline Sports Academy (TSA) is Dubai’s leading youth football academy, dedicated to delivering professional, structured, and fun football training for boys and girls aged 4–18+. Located inside Al Ahli Club in Al Nahda 1, TSA combines expert coaching, modern outdoor facilities, and a player‑first philosophy to nurture each athlete’s technical skills, physical fitness, and mental confidence.

Our age‑specific programs use proven development pathways—from foundational ball mastery at U6 to competitive tactics for U14+—so every child advances at the right pace. Under the guidance of UEFA‑licensed and locally accredited coaches, players learn teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership on and off the pitch. Whether your child is beginning their football journey or aiming for the next level, TSA’s supportive environment and clear progression roadmap ensure measurable improvement and lifelong love of the game.

Expert Coaching

UEFA‑qualified, background‑checked coaches with years of youth and grassroots experience.

Structured Pathways

Clear progression from U6 fundamentals through U14+ competitive tactics.

Top‑Tier Facilities
