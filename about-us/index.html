WHO WE ARE

More Than a Football
Academy — A Community

Touchline Sports Academy (TSA) is Dubai's leading youth football academy, dedicated to delivering professional, structured, and fun football training for boys and girls aged 4–18+. Located inside Al Ahli Club in Al Nahda 1 and International Academy in Al Warqa, TSA combines expert coaching, modern outdoor facilities, and a player-first philosophy.

Our age-specific programs use proven development pathways — from foundational ball mastery at U6 to competitive tactics for U14+ — so every child advances at the right pace. Under the guidance of licensed and locally accredited coaches, players learn teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership on and off the pitch.

OUR JOURNEY

The Touchline Story

From a single pitch to Dubai's most trusted football academy — here's our journey so far.

2024 — The Beginning

Touchline is Born

Touchline Sports Academy opens its doors at Al Ahli Club in Al Nahda 1, Dubai, with a bold vision: deliver world-class football coaching to every child in the UAE.

2024 — Growth

100+ Families Join

Within months of launch, over 100 families from across Dubai enrolled their children, with the academy earning a 4.9-star Google rating from day one.

2025 — Expansion

Al Warqa Branch Opens

To serve East Dubai, we open our second venue inside International Academy, Al Warqa 1 — bringing our premium coaching closer to families in Mirdif, Nad Al Hamar, and Rashidiya.

2025 — Today

200+ Players Strong

Today, Touchline proudly serves 200+ active players across all age groups — U6 to Adults, Girls, Corporate, and Personal Coaching — becoming a cornerstone of Dubai's football community.
