Why Register with Touchline Sports Academy?

Registration FAQ

What age groups do you accept?

We welcome players from age 3 (Pre-U6 program) through to adults (16+ years). Our programs include U6, U8, U10, U12, U14+, Girls Football (all ages), Adult Training, and Personal Coaching.

What happens after I submit this form?

Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your enquiry and schedule your FREE trial session. You'll receive all the details about which program is right for your child, session timings, and what to bring.

Is the trial session really free?

Yes, absolutely! We offer a completely free trial session with no hidden fees or commitment required. It's the perfect way to try out our coaching before enrolling.

Where are your training locations in Dubai?

We train at two locations in Dubai: (1) Al Nahda — Inside Al Ahli Club, Al Nahda 1, behind Al Mulla Plaza, near Stadium Metro Station. (2) Al Warqa — Inside International Academy, Al Warqa 1. We also operate in Sharjah.

Do you offer corporate and adult football programs?

Yes! We offer Adult Football Training (16+ years), Personal 1-on-1 Coaching, and Corporate Team Training programs. These are available separately from youth programs. Select "Adults Football Program" or "Corporate Training / Tie-up" from the enquiry type dropdown in the form.
